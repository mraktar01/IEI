Our Story

From a Small Dream to a Big Movement

IEI was founded by a group of educators, professionals, and social workers who saw brilliant children dropping out of school due to poverty — not lack of talent.

Starting with just scholarship recipients in Malda district, we have grown into a movement that reaches over many villages, and inspires communities to invest in education.
