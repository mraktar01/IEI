Our Story

From a Small Dream to a Big Movement

IEI was founded by a group of educators, professionals, and social workers who saw brilliant children dropping out of school due to poverty — not lack of talent. That injustice drove them to act.

Starting with just scholarships recipients in Malda district, we have grown into a movement that reaches over many vilages, and inspires communities to invest in education.

In these days IEI wants to spread scholarship programs, and create digital literacy centers, innovation labs, and focus to the girl child education initiatives — all aimed at one goal: Ensuring no child is left behind because of financial hardship.
